The Best Movies According to Angela

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Ludo (2020)

A quirky and colorful film where four different stories connect through fate, crime, and chaos — all tied together like a game of Ludo.

Tumbbad (2018)

A dark fantasy-horror tale about a man’s endless greed as he discovers a hidden treasure guarded by an ancient, cursed being in a mysterious village.

3 Idiots (2009)

A heartwarming and funny story of three engineering students navigating college, friendship, and pressure — with an important message: follow your passion.
